About Us

Grocareer TRAININGs and placements is an exclusive placement portal for skilled and unskilled professionals. We are having tie-ups with small, midsize and with MNCs (National and International) wise. We will be getting lot of requirements from our clients (employers) to supply skilled and unskilled professionals.

If requires as a part of placements, grocareer itself gives TRAININGs as per the requirements received from clients (employers) and make them to face the interviews as per industry standards. Also we give free career council to the candidates so that candidates can improve their skill-set as per industry standards and can settle down in their career.

grocareer is having industry experienced and specialized team in all the domains who will work on each individual resume and guide the candidates properly to settle down in their career as per their education and skill-set.

grocareer will work in 2-way-functionality. It will help the unemployees to get employment in big companies and for employees to show better employment, at the same time it will help their clients (employers) to get skilled employees as per their requirements.

Hence grocareer is a “One Stop Solution” for Unemployees, Employees and for Employers as we have tie-up with small size, midsize, multinational companies, and with professional educational institutions.
