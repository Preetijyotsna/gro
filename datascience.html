DataScience Overview

NEXT GENERATION Data Science is the Future, the U.S. Bureau of Labour Statistics reports SAYS that the rise of data science demand will create 11.5 Million job openings by next coming years. Not only that there is a huge demand, but there is also a noticeable shortage of qualified data scientists. The IT industry is expecting to add around 180000–200000 fresh job vacancies that are related to recent technologies like Data Science (Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning and Machine Learning). This job profile offers great opportunities to fresher’s who have the relevant skills and they have an extremely bright future ahead. A large no. of Indian developers is running towards data science since it the most in-trend job role and is expected to have a great future ahead as well.


Keeping the Data Science future in mind grocareer has introduced the below courses:
- Machine Learning (ML)
- Deep Learning (DL)
- Artificial Intelligence (AI)
- R Programming
- Python
- Big-Data - Hadoop
- Robotics
- Natural Language Processing (NLP)
- Statistical Data Analysis
- Etc.,

A popular staffing solutions co., and other famous sources says by the year “2020, India will face a demand-supply gap of 2,00,000 data analytics professionals” So, if taken in the right way, this career path can yield amazing and successful results for a candidate who aspires to work with numbers, has good analytical and logical skills, and wants good salaries and growth opportunities. But for that you need to know what skills are actually in high demand in the IT industry currently. These are the skills that you should have in you to get hired as a data scientist:

Statistics and Statistical Analysis, Predictive Modelling, R & Python, Data Cleaning, Machine Learning, Text Mining, Visualisation, Deployment, Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning, Big data, Hadoop, etc.,

If you do not have these skills, getting hired in data science roles would be extremely challenging. Each of these skills requires extensive experience before you get on to work on real data science projects. These skills come from a diverse set of background of statistics, computer science, mathematics, so you can imagine how challenging it can be to a master them.

Machine learning algorithms, and especially within the subfield of Deep Learning, Artificial Intelligence have advanced rapidly in the last few years. In addition, there is intense development of Machine Learning software. This is improving the quality of the algorithms and making the tools easier to use, lowering the barriers to entry for aspiring data scientists. Because of the strong dependency on Machine learning tools, advancements in this field directly influence the usefulness and capabilities of Data Science.

Overall, if you want to master the above skills, you should learn while working on live projects. Grocareer will help their students to learn from highly experience Industry legends and gives an opportunity to work on live projects with job placements.
